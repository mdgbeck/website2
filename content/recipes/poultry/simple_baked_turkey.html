Simple Roasted Turkey

Originally from: Food Wishes

Ingredients

  • 12 - 24 lb turkey
  • salt
  • pepper
  • cayenne pepper
  • poultry seasoning
  • 3 Tbl butter
  • fresh herbs (sage, rosemary, thyme)
  • 1 onion
  • 2 celery ribs
  • 2 carrots

Directions

  1. Defrost turkey and remove all trimmed parts.
  2. Place in roasting pan on top of chopped onion, carrots, and celery. Tuck wingtips under breasts.
  3. Melt butter and add herbs for a minute as butter starts to turn gold. Place herbs inside cavity of turkey.
  4. Brush herb butter all over turkey and then cover in seasonings.
  5. Bake in 325° until middle thigh reads 170 - 175° about 15 minutes per pound.

Notes

  • 2020-11-26 - Baked turkey using this method for 2020 thanksgiving. Turkey was still slightly frozen when going into oven. Worked well - very easy and turkey was still decently moist and tasty at end with no other basting or anything fancy. As we did not have great roasting pan I placed veggies in 9x13 pan and then set wired rack on top with turkey. Also worked pretty well, probably only doable with smaller turkey to make sure to catch all drippings. Drippings went into gravy and that was good too.
