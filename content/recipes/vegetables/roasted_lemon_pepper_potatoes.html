Roasted Lemon Pepper Potatoes

Originally from: Food Wishes

Ingredients

    4 large russet potatoes 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper to taste Â¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper, or to taste 1 pinch kosher salt to taste 1/4 cup olive oil, or more to taste 1 cup chicken broth 3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, or to taste 2 teaspoons lemon zest 4 sprigs fresh oregano leaves, or more to taste

Directions

  1. Cut off long ends of potatoes so they will lay flat - cut in half lengthwise, rinse and dry.
  2. Place potatoes in oiled baking dish, brushing top with oil as well. Season with salt and peppers.
  3. Bake in 400° oven for 20 minutes. Remove and flip potatoes, let rest.
  4. Combine broth, lemon zest and juice. Pour over potatoes and brush any zest off top of potatoes. Stick oregano between potatoes.
  5. Return to oven and continue to roast flipping every 15 minutes, until desired doneness - 30 - 45 more minutes or so.
  6. Drizzle more oil and season as needed to serve.

Notes

  • 2020-08-30 - made following recipe without oregano sprigs. Were decent - probably could have used additional roasting time to develop more crispness. 1 cup broth seemed a little much for amount of potatoes in 9 x 13 pan, but did mostly cook off, but may have increased cooking time.
