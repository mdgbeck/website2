Chinese-American Tofu and Broccoli

Adapted From Serious Eats: link

Ingredients

  • Extra-firm tofu
  • Chopped Broccoli Florets
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sugar
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Sesame Oil
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Onion

Directions

  1. Press and dry tofu, cut in pieces
  2. Heat oil in wok and cook tofu, adding soy sauce. Set aside when cooked.
  3. Add garlic and ginger, cook until start to brown. Add broccoli and onion. Let cook a few minutes, then add remaining sauce ingredients. When almost done, add in cooked tofu.
  4. Serve over rice.

Notes
