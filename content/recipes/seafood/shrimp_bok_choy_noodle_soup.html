Shrimp and Bok Choy Noodle Soup

Originally From: NA

Ingredients

  • Rice Noodle (Pho style)
  • Shrimp
  • Chicken Broth / Stock
  • Soy Sauce
  • Fish Sauce
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Cilantro
  • Carrots
  • Baby Bok Choy
  • Red Onion
  • Jalepeño

Directions

  1. Make broth base of soup, if making from "scratch". Add soy and fish sauce.
  2. Add carrots, sliced / chopped ginger, garlic, and cilantro. Cook 5-10 minutes
  3. Follow noodle directions - likely quick bath in boiling water then set aside
  4. Add bok choy and any jalepeño you want cooked to soup. Cook another few minutes
  5. Add shrimp. Once shrimp cooked through, serve over prepared noodles. Top with onion, jalepeño, cilantro, and lime.

Notes

  • 2020-06-14 - Made with Fresh Rice Sitck Noodles (Sincere Orient Food Co.). Tasted ok, broth was pretty plain, needed less water / more flavor / simmer longer. Noodles, shrimp, bok choy all good texture / flavor.
