Turmeric Rice

Originally from: Serious Eats

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbl butter
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/4 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 1/2 cups rice
  • 2 1/2 cups chicken broth

Directions

  1. Melt butter in pot, add spices and cook until fragrant.
  2. Add rice and cook until lightly toasted, about 4 minutes.
  3. Add chicken broth and some salt / pepper. Bring to boil.
  4. Cover, then reduce heat to low. Cook for 15 minutes and remove from heat. Let rest until water is gone and rice is tender, about another 15 minutes.

Notes

  • 2020-11-22 - Recipe works well, I've added chopped onions sometimes. Nothing too strong or different, but gets nice rice with good yellow color.
