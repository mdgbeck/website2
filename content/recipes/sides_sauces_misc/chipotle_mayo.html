Chipotle Mayo

Adapted from: Serious Eats

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 3/4 cup vegetable Oil
  • 1 chipotle pepper in adobo sauce
  • ~3 Tbl sour cream
  • salt

Directions

  1. Use a cup that just fits immeresion blender. Add egg, lemon juice, garlic, chipotle pepper with some sauce to cup.
  2. Add oil to cup and wait until settled. Insert blender, and turn on high speed letting blender draw oil to bottom. After 10 seconds or so slowly lift blender out, continuing to mix allowing top oil to emulsify as well.
  3. Stir in sour cream, and add salt to taste.

Notes

  • 2020-06-20 - Turned out pretty good - 1 chipotle pepper had decent flavor and some spice, but might be worth using two in future.
