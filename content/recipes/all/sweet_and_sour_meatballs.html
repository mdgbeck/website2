Sweet and Sour Meatballs

Originally from: Lionhouse Recipes Book

Ingredients

Meatballs

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 3/4 cup rolled oats
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup milk

Sauce

  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 1 tsp yellow mustard
  • 1/4 cup bbq sauce
  • 1 tsp worchestershire sauce

Directions

  1. Combine all sauce ingredients and heat on stove.
  2. Combine all meatball ingredients and form into meatballs.
  3. Place meatballs in baking dish (I've used pyrex normally) and cover with sauce.
  4. Bake in 350° oven for 30 minutes.

Notes

  • 2021-01-03 Made as above using sweet baby ray honey bbq sauce. Turned out fine, beef was 80-20 which left some floating grease in sauce, might be best to use leaner beef in future.
