Grilled Lemon Garlic Shrimp

Originally from: all recipes

Ingredients

  • 1 pound frozen shrimp, thawed and peeled
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 2 teaspoons lemon juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 pinch finely chopped parsley
  • metal skewers
  • cooking spray

Directions

  1. Defrost shrimp if using frozen.
  2. Combine all ingredients and marinate for 2 hours.
  3. Cook on grill.

Notes
