Hamburger Buns

Originally from: Food Wishes

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 tsp yeast
  • 1 pound all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 Tbl butter
  • 3 Tbl sugar
  • 1 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 Tbl milk
  • seasame seeds

Directions

  1. Make dough from all ingredients except milk, 1 egg, and sesame seeds. Dough should be sticky, but should not stick to fingers when poking. Let rise until doubled in size.
  2. Punch down and turn out dough onto floured surface and divide into 8 pieces.
  3. Form each piece into round disc, about 1/2 inch tall. Place onto baking sheet with parchement paper, or silicone mat. Let rise again until doubled in size.
  4. Make egg wash with additional egg and milk, and spread gently on buns after they have risen. Sprinkle sesame seeds on top.
  5. Bake in 375° oven 15 - 17 minutes.

Notes

  • 2020-06-20 - Made according to recipe sans sesame seeds. Turned out ok, think I cut the second rise a little too short, and over flattened discs. Buns were too thin, but still had decent taste / texture and larger ones held up to good size burgers.
