Pad See Ew

Originally from: Hot Thai Kitchen

Ingredients

Main:

  • 170g (6oz) protein of your choice, thinly sliced (see note)
  • 1 Tbsp soy sauce for marinating meat, omit if using shrimp or tofu
  • 3-4 Tbsp vegetable oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 2 eggs
  • 4-6 stalks Chinese broccoli (use 6 stalks if they're small)
  • 1 lb (450g) fresh wide rice noodles, store bought (see note) or make your own
  • 4 tsp sugar
  • Ground white pepper, to taste

Sauce

  • 2 Tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 1/2 tsp fish sauce
  • 1 1/2 tsp Golden Mountain sauce (can sub soy)
  • 2 tsp Thai black soy sauce

Directions

  1. Stir meat with soy sauce to marinate.
  2. Combine all sauce ingredients.
  3. Heat oil is wok over medium high heat and cook meat until done.
  4. Consider dividing dish into portions and repeat the following steps to avoid over-crowding
  5. Sauté garlic until start to color.
  6. Add egg and scramble.
  7. Add Broccoli and cook 30 seconds.
  8. Add noodles, sauce and sugar. Turn heat to high and stir, coating everything in sauce.
  9. Spread noodles out across pan and let cook until some begin to char. Flip and repeat.
  10. Add protein back into pan and stir.

Notes
