Honey Mustard Roast Chicken

Originally from: Food Wishes

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup yellow mustard
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup seasoned rice wine vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons Sriracha
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 3 teaspoons kosher salt, plus more as needed
  • 1 (4.5 pound) whole chicken
  • 1/4 cup chicken stock, or as needed
  • 2 tablespoons chopped green onions, or to taste

Directions

  1. Mix all ingredients except stock and green onion together. Marinate chicken for 4 - 12 hours.
  2. Bake chicken on foil lined baking sheet at 425° for 35 - 40 minutes.
  3. Take excess marinade and bring to a boil adding stock as necessary to thin.
  4. Baste chicken with sauce as desired and cook until chicken is done.

Notes

  • 2020-08-30 - Cooked using 4 chicken quarters - turned out pretty good. Maybe use less mustard and more honey in future.
