Quick Garlic Breadsticks

Originally adapted from: 'http://blessmynest.blogspot.com/' (blog dead)

Ingredients

  • 4 cups all purpose flour (500 g)
  • 2.5 tsp salt
  • 2 Tbl sugar
  • 1.5 cups water
  • 2 1/4 tsp yeast
  • 2 Tbl butter
  • 6 Tbl garlic butter

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients except garlic butter and knead until smooth
  2. Separate into 12 portions. Roll into breadsticks, or tie for garlic knots.
  3. Let rise for 30 - 45 minutes. Preheat oven to 400°
  4. Bake for 5 - 7 minutes then brush with garlic butter. Bake additional couple minutes, remove and brush with remaining garlic butter.
